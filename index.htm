Welcome! On this page I will be giving you some general information about Lake Tahoe and why it is a great spot to go on vacation.

As you can see from the picture above, Lake Tahoe is a wonderful destination for a vacation! There are scenic trails, beaches, and hikes to go on while visiting. The Rubicon Trail is regarded as one of the best trails to go on due to its amazing views of the lake, distant mountains, and the shallow bays. The 1 most recommended beach site at Lake Tahoe, is the Sand Harbor Beach. The area includes a vistior center, picnic tables, trees, and a wide stratch of sunny sand. There is even a beach and cove specifically set up for SCUBA Divers!
